# PageKind Art Direction

## Overview

Each of the 7 page kinds has distinct art direction governing its background image. The art direction defines the visual role, safe zone geometry, and stylistic intent.

## Page Kinds

### 1. cover
- **Visual Role**: hero-impact
- **Safe Zone**: Center 60% width, 40-80% height (title + subtitle region)
- **Art Direction**: Bold, atmospheric, brand-anchoring. Single dominant visual element. Maximum emotional impact with minimal clutter. The background sets the tone for the entire deck.

### 2. executive-summary
- **Visual Role**: ambient-authority
- **Safe Zone**: Left 65% width, full height (text-heavy region)
- **Art Direction**: Subdued, professional gradient or abstract pattern. Must convey competence and clarity. Background should not compete with dense text content.

### 3. problem-analysis
- **Visual Role**: tension-signal
- **Safe Zone**: Top 30% + bottom 20% reserved for headers/footers; central area shared with data
- **Art Direction**: Subtle visual tension (diagonal lines, contrasting zones). Supports narrative of challenge/opportunity without being alarming. Cool or neutral tones preferred.

### 4. comparison
- **Visual Role**: structured-compare
- **Safe Zone**: Two vertical halves, each with 70% inner safe zone for comparison columns
- **Art Direction**: Symmetrical or split-field composition. Clear visual separation between comparison sides. Neutral bridge zone in center. Minimal decorative elements.

### 5. roadmap
- **Visual Role**: forward-flow
- **Safe Zone**: Central horizontal band (30-70% height) for timeline; margins for labels
- **Art Direction**: Directional flow (left-to-right or bottom-to-top). Abstract path, gradient progression, or horizon metaphor. Conveys forward momentum and structured planning.

### 6. deep-dive
- **Visual Role**: detail-canvas
- **Safe Zone**: 80% of total area (dense content page)
- **Art Direction**: Minimal, almost invisible background. Extremely low noise budget. Subtle texture or very light gradient only. Content is king on this page.

### 7. decision-cta
- **Visual Role**: action-anchor
- **Safe Zone**: Upper 50% for summary text, lower 30% for CTA button/action area
- **Art Direction**: Convergent composition drawing eye toward action zone. Warm or energetic accent permitted. Background should create a sense of resolution and forward commitment.

| PageKind | Safe Zone Coverage | Noise Budget | Contrast Budget |
|----------|-------------------|--------------|-----------------|
